Roleplay Rules

General Rules

  • Required Age: 18+ for newcomers
  • A working, high-quality microphone is required
  • Your character must have a reasonably realistic name and must not violate any Terms of Service (TOS)
  • All players must use a customized character and are NOT permitted to use a PED unless whitelisted for roleplaying that specific PED character
  • Players must adhere to all Kick, TikTok, and Twitch Terms of Service (TOS) at all times
  • You must be equipped with some form of gameplay recording software with audio capability (e.g., OBS, Medal.tv, Twitch, NVIDIA, etc.)
  • Exhibiting or fostering toxicity on any platform (including Twitch, Reddit, Discord, etc.) may lead to removal from the community at any time
  • Role-play involving rape or sexual harassment is prohibited. If someone feels uncomfortable with the statements being made, they may report it to the Staff
  • No instances of homophobia, racism, real-life threats, or harassment of any form will be tolerated
  • Impersonation of Staff members is prohibited
  • Cheating or hacking in any form will result in a permanent ban
  • Crimes against EMS personnel when they are clocked in and on duty are not allowed
  • Mod packs that provide any advantage are prohibited. Tracer bullets, sound effect kills, and disabling smoke/flash effects are not permitted on the server
  • Respect towards all Staff is mandatory at all times. Disrespectful or aggressive behavior towards Staff will not be tolerated
  • Do not disclose details of tickets to anyone
  • Refrain from specifically naming Staff members in public forums
  • Staff members involved in a reported scene will NOT be allowed to review the ticket
  • All Reports/Punishments are reviewed and approved by multiple Staff members. Requesting a different Staff member simply because you disagree with a decision is not allowed
  • Permanent bans can be appealed after 30 days

Green Zones

  • There are no green zones in Life Roleplay. Please understand that shooting in active businesses or government buildings has consequences. Refrain from shooting in those businesses unless the roleplay requires it.

Rogue Employee RP

  • Rogue Employee RP is ILLEGAL for EVERYONE.

In-Game Information

Always Stay in Character [A]

  • Address any issues by fully role-playing out the scenario, and if necessary, file a player report afterward
  • Refrain from breaking character or informing players that they're breaking a rule in-game
  • Do not carry out actions with the intent of provoking others into breaking character

Appropriate Terminology [A]

  • 'Locals' refer to NPCs in LC
  • 'Downed' indicates that your character is incapacitated or severely injured and requires EMS revival
  • 'Scuffed' is used when something is malfunctioning or glitched
  • 'Cough Drop / Clear Your Throat' is a phrase to fix voice chat issues
  • 'Headache / Head Popped' signifies game performance issues or crashes
  • 'Tsunami / Thunder Storming' indicates a server restart
  • 'Take a nap' means reconnecting to the server
  • 'Spider' describes a player animation glitch
  • 'Thinking / Meditating' is code for going AFK
  • 'Going to Sleep' means logging off for an extended period
  • 'Voices / Angels' are terms for Twitch Chat or background noise
  • 'The Government' references the Management and Staff team

/OOC Chat Usage [A]

  • OOC is not tolerated at all. Please finish your scenes and, if necessary, make a ticket accordingly. Use the above terminology to refrain from OOC.

Server Restarts [A]

  • Normal role-play should not degrade in quality or cease before a restart; all scenarios should continue as usual
  • Avoid petty crimes immediately before or after a restart
  • Heists (bank, jewelry, etc.) should not be initiated 30 minutes prior to or post-restart
  • Refrain from crafting or transferring items to or from storage or inventory shortly before or after a restart

Combat Logging [A]

  • Exiting the game intentionally to evade the consequences of your in-game actions is prohibited. If you're disconnected due to unforeseen reasons, you must return to your last location and conclude the given scene, calling EMS if necessary.

Loot Boxing [A]

  • Robbing another player must involve sufficient role-play, with required interaction before any hostile acts like robbery, kidnapping, or shooting.

Cop Baiting [A]

  • Committing crimes with the intention of drawing police attention is not allowed, such as speeding past officers, ignoring traffic signals, or committing multiple visible crimes.

Metagaming [D]

  • It is forbidden to use or share information in-game that your character did not learn through in-game channels (this includes Twitch Chats and Discord Channels). Using external calls (like Xbox Live Party, Discord, etc.) while in the city is considered metagaming.

Power Gaming/Fail RP [B]

  • You should not utilize out-of-game methods to gain advantages or fail to blend your actions within the game world naturally. Avoid communicating, organizing, or revealing locations while incapacitated. Don't focus exclusively on money-making without integrating role-play elements around that mechanic.

Usage of /me and /do Commands

  • /Me displays an action or visible emotion of a character
  • /Do describes the state of the character and surrounding objects

Random Death Match (RDM) & Vehicle Death Match (VDM) [B or C Depending on the Situation]

  • There must be a significant storyline or backstory before committing crimes against another player. This means you may not rob or kill anyone you are meeting for the first time. If you are being antagonized and have an interaction the first time you meet, you may commit crimes. Interaction time is voided if there is significant backstory to commit a crime against someone.
  • RDM: Attacking another player/group without any story or roleplay leading up to it.
  • VDM: Attacking another player/group using a vehicle with the intent to kill/knock them out. Vehicles cannot be used as weapons.
  • Speed (100+) attacking other vehicles (ramming) is not allowed.
  • No civilians or criminals are to use pit maneuvering of any kind, but you can create roadblocks using cars.

New Life Rule (NLR) [B]

  • If you are downed and respawn at the hospital, your character forgets the last 45 minutes and all events leading up to being downed in the current scenario. You may not respawn if you’ve been advised that police or EMS is on the way to your scene. You must try to contact medical help through your phone or in-character communication when incapacitated.
  • If you are found on your side and calling for help on the scene, you may remember but may not interact with anyone around you. If you are found on your back with flat lying animation, you may not talk or interact with the scene.
  • You may not return to the scene if you respawned at the hospital. You must wait for a minimum of 45 minutes from injury/respawn before remembering anything.

Multiple Characters

  • Players are not allowed to have multiple characters in the same gang.

Not Valuing Life (NVL) [C or D Depending on the Situation]

  • You must realistically fear for your life at all times and act accordingly. You cannot run into a gunfight without fearing for your character's life. Failure to do so will lead to disciplinary action.
  • Shootouts should not occur at or around PD or hospitals. All shootouts must take place in realistic and appropriate locations.
  • You may not tackle during an active shootout.
  • Antagonizing groups, police, or individuals without an in-character reason is not allowed.

Criminal Activities [B]

  • There can only be 5 criminals (max) involved in a criminal activity at a time.
  • You must wait 2 minutes after completing a robbery before you can engage with law enforcement. This means no shooting, no carjacking, no robbing, etc.
  • You cannot commit crimes against the same player within 45 minutes.
  • Weapons are not allowed to be smuggled into the jail unless it is a pre-organized jailbreak.
  • You cannot use your own prospects (gang members) as hostages.
  • Frequent, repetitive, or severe acts of violence against other players are prohibited.
  • Kill on Sight (KOS) is prohibited, and any act of violence must be preceded by a full interaction between all parties involved.
  • Robbing or stealing PD/EMS items (including vehicles) while they are on duty is not allowed.

Gang Strike System

  • First Strike: Documented warning on record and official gang lead notification.
  • Second Strike: Further actions will be taken, including but not limited to temporary bans of leaders and members.
  • Third Strike: Final warning, gang termination, and potential full character CK.

Gang Rules

  • Gangs must have no more than 15 members.
  • All gangs or criminal organizations must be approved by Staff. The same applies to legal organizations.
  • New affiliates cannot participate in an official capacity until vetted and authorized.
  • Public areas (e.g., beaches, parks) cannot be claimed by any gang.
  • Gangs may tax others if they own territory with sprays.
  • Players can only have one character per gang. No exceptions.
  • Beef between gangs/organizations must have a detailed storyline.
  • To press other gangs, your gang must be verified.

Bleedouts/CK Contracts

  • Bleedouts are allowed. Gang Leaders must have proof of a signed CK contract.

Gang Wars Protocol

  • Gang wars must be conducted in the city in a controlled environment with Staff oversight.
  • Location, date, and time must be agreed upon by gang leaders involved.
  • KOS is allowed within the restricted location, date, and time of the gang war.
  • No more than 15 participants per gang during the gang war.
  • Gangs cannot form alliances during a gang war.
  • Police will not intervene in official gang wars.
  • Gangs must wear their complete uniform and correct color during the gang war.

Gang Wars Conclusion

  • The war ends when all members of the opposing gang are dead or incapacitated.
  • The losing gang must pay the specified amount agreed upon before the war.
  • There is a cooldown period of 7 days before initiating another war.

Robberies

Bank Robberies

  • 2 to 5 robbers allowed
  • 2 or more hostages, negotiations can be for up to 3
  • Free passage is not given; you must roleplay negotiations
  • Only 1 getaway vehicle is allowed; another vehicle may be parked elsewhere
  • No outside shooters are allowed

Jewelry Store Robberies

  • 2 to 4 robbers allowed
  • 1 or more hostages, negotiations can be for up to 2
  • Free passage is not given; you must roleplay negotiations
  • Only 1 getaway vehicle is allowed; another vehicle may be parked elsewhere
  • No outside shooters are allowed

House Robberies

  • 1 to 2 robbers allowed
  • Only 2 people per house; no simultaneous house robbing
  • No outside shooters are allowed

Convenience Store Robberies

  • 2 robbers allowed
  • 1 to 2 hostages
  • Free passage is not given; you must roleplay negotiations
  • Only 1 getaway vehicle is allowed; another vehicle may be parked elsewhere
  • No outside shooters are allowed

Car Boosting

  • You cannot return to the contract location until you have lost the police
  • Boosting contracts can be done solo or with a partner (max 2 people)
